About Me

Hi, My name is Balaji Bharatwaj and I am, yet another undergrad student of Amrita University, Coimbatore. I am passionate about cybersecurity and want to make that as my career, but web design and blogs are my hobbies.
I love learning new stuff. But I love sharing stuff even more. When I decided that I wanted to share my views and my experiences, I was preparing myself as to how to deliver my content to my audience. Initially, I wanted to do a podcast, but my weakness is that I talk so fast when I understand something down to the core. My main intention is to make sure my audience understands what I think, and realize that my perspective exists as well.
So that's why I am starting this blog series called "My Thoughts". I am excited to share my views. A new blog every 2 weeks. There are going to be blogs on new technology, important issues around us and some of my personal experiences. Feel free to hit me up on any one of the Social Media. Or send me an email to mbalaji.manikandan@gmail.com for any suggestions, or topics you think I wanna talk about. Once again, Thank you so much for taking the initiative to read my blogs.
